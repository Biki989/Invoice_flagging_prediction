Platform Overview

Freight & Invoice Intelligence Suite

InvoiceGuard is an ML-powered analytics platform built for enterprise procurement and finance teams to automate cost benchmarking and compliance screening at scale. The platform provides two core capabilities: a regression model that predicts expected freight costs from invoice values, helping procurement managers validate shipping charges before purchase order approval; and a binary classifier that screens vendor invoices in real time, surfacing entries that deviate from established spending patterns for manual audit review. Both models are optimised for low-latency inference, delivering predictions in under 100 ms to integrate seamlessly into existing approval workflows.
